What to Expect

Whether you're visiting for the first time or a lifelong member, here's what our services look like

🕊️

Reverent Worship

Our liturgy follows ancient Coptic rites with hymns, prayers, and readings that have been preserved for nearly 2,000 years.

🎵

Beautiful Hymns

Experience the rich Coptic hymnal tradition, with melodies passed down from the early Egyptian Church.

📜

Scripture & Sermon

Every service includes readings from the Old Testament, Epistles, Acts, and the Gospel, followed by a sermon.

🍞

Holy Communion

The Eucharist is the center of our liturgy — the Body and Blood of Christ offered to all baptized Orthodox believers.

Special Services & Seasons

Throughout the liturgical year, we observe special feasts and fasting seasons

Great Lent

The 55-day fasting period before Easter (Pascha), with intensified prayer and liturgies throughout the week.

Holy Week (Pascha)

The most sacred week in the Coptic calendar, commemorating the Passion, Crucifixion, and Resurrection of Christ.

Feast of Nativity

Christmas celebration on January 7th, preceded by the Advent fast, with a beautiful midnight liturgy.

Feast Days of Saints

Special liturgies and celebrations honoring the saints, including our patron St. Mina the Wonder Worker.
