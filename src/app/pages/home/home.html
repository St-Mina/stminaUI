A Church Rooted in
Faith & Tradition

St. Mina Coptic Orthodox Church is a vibrant community of believers, united in the ancient faith passed down from the Apostles. Our church welcomes all who seek to grow closer to God through liturgy, sacraments, and fellowship.

Named after the great Egyptian martyr St. Mina the Wonder Worker, our parish strives to embody his courage and devotion in our modern lives.
